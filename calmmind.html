Mindfulness Meditation

pick one of your five senses to focus on for this meditation.
senses such as sight that give a lot of information may be more difficult to work with, but can be good practice.

focus on your sense. notice all of the information it's giving you. describe to yourself everything your sense is telling you.
if your mind wanders or goes to other thoughts that is okay, just acknowledge this and bring your focus back to your sense.
pick out a single piece of information to focus on. is it the sound of rain or cars? the texture of fabric? what is the source?

continue to focus on your sense until you feel that you meditation is complete.
it is best to practice when undisturbed but it is possible to practice anytime and anywhere, should you choose to do so.
